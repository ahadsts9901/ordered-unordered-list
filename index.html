How To Make Kheer?

This Is The Easy Way To Make Kheer At Home

Ingredients

  • Rice (1/4 KG)
  • Sugar (1/4 KG)
  • Milk (1 Litre)
  • Dry Fruits(Almond,Pista,Coconuts)
  • Water (According to need)

Steps For Cooking

  1. Boil the rice in water
  2. Boil milk with sugar
  3. Add the boiled milk in boiled rice
  4. Grind it well
  5. Pour this in a bowl
  6. Add dry fruits and enjoy
